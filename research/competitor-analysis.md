# Competitor Analysis

## Existing Categories

- Space situational awareness providers.
- Conjunction alerting systems.
- Government and agency collision-assessment workflows.
- Satellite operator internal tools.

## OrbitGuard Differentiation

- Goes beyond alerting into maneuver recommendation.
- Includes secondary conjunction screening after a proposed maneuver.
- Uses explainable, audit-style decision output.
- Provides deterministic public demo scenarios.
- Targets accessibility for students, small operators, and judges.

## Positioning Risk

Judges may ask whether this already exists. The response:

> Existing systems monitor and alert. OrbitGuard demonstrates the full autonomous copilot loop: detect, decide, plan, re-screen, explain, and report.

## Claims To Avoid

- Do not claim operational certification.
- Do not claim exact collision probability from TLE-only data.
- Do not claim replacement of professional SSA systems.
