# OrbitGuard Demo Script

## Primary Video Target

Length: 3 minutes.

Goal: make judges remember that OrbitGuard does not just warn about risk; it clears risk with an explainable maneuver.

## Shot List

### 0:00-0:20 Hook

Open `/`. Earth appears in a starfield with visible satellite and debris models.

Narration: "Space is no longer empty. OrbitGuard protects a working satellite from debris before the close approach becomes a crisis."

### 0:20-0:45 Problem

Open `/mission`. Show the protected asset, threat object, closest approach, collision probability, and one Plan Avoidance action.

Narration: "Operators receive warnings, but the hard question is what to do next. A bad maneuver can waste fuel or create a new risk."

### 0:45-1:15 Protect ISRO

Open `/catalog`. Show CARTOSAT-2F and expand raw TLE evidence.

Narration: "The data is inspectable. OrbitGuard keeps the source object, owner, orbit class, and raw TLE evidence available without overwhelming the operator."

### 1:15-1:50 Detail and Risk

Open `/risk`. Show closest approach and collision probability explanations, then scenario ranking.

Narration: "The system explains the geometry and risk assumptions in plain language before exposing the technical numbers."

### 1:50-2:25 Copilot Maneuver

Open `/avoidance`. Click Run Burn Scan and Apply Recommendation. Show before/after risk collapse.

Narration: "The Copilot evaluates 18 candidate burns and selects a 0.12 meter-per-second along-track maneuver four hours before closest approach."

### 2:25-2:45 Secondary Screening

Show the secondary screening clear state on `/avoidance`.

Narration: "Then it re-checks the new orbit so the dodge does not create a different problem."

### 2:45-3:00 Close

Open `/reports`. Generate the briefing and export Markdown.

Narration: "OrbitGuard makes space safety explainable, accessible, and actionable. Don't just see the risk. Clear it."
