# Glossary

## TLE

Two-Line Element set. A public orbital element format used with SGP4 propagation.

## SGP4

Standard propagator used with TLEs to estimate satellite position and velocity over time.

## Conjunction

A close approach between two orbital objects.

## TCA

Time of Closest Approach.

## Miss Distance

The closest predicted distance between two objects during a conjunction.

## Pc

Estimated probability of collision. In OrbitGuard MVP, Pc is computed from documented assumptions and must not be presented as operational-grade certainty.

## Delta-v

Change in velocity used to maneuver a spacecraft.

## Secondary Screening

Checking whether a proposed avoidance maneuver creates a new conjunction risk.

## Scenario Mode

Deterministic demo mode using controlled fixtures rather than relying on live catalog events.
